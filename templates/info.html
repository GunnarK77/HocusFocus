About HocusFocus

HocusFocus is designed to help you understand and improve your smartphone usage habits. Our app uses simulations to demonstrate how small changes in your daily routine can lead to significant improvements in productivity and focus.

How It Works

Our predefined simulation shows the potential impact of using HocusFocus for different types of users over a 7-day period. It tracks:

  • Total screen time
  • Social media usage
  • Reading time
  • Points earned for positive behaviors

By visualizing these trends, you can see how gradual changes in habits can lead to more balanced and productive smartphone use.
